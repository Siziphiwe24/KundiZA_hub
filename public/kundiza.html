IBM KundiZA Hub

IBM KundiZA Hub, situated at 90 Grayston Drive in Sandton – the Heart of Africa’s business district – offers an exceptional location for Collaboration and Innovation. With stunning views of Sandton, the hub is conveniently within walking distance of key destinations and provides a shuttle service to and from the Gautrain Station, Sandton City, Nelson Mandela Square, and Benmore Gardens. The building proudly holds a 4-star Green Star rating, reflecting its commitment to sustainability and minimizing its environmental impact.

The theatre-style room is designed with comfortable seating to ensure attendees remain at ease throughout their sessions.
